Piceno LLC is licensed to import wines by the United States federal government.

Additionally, Piceno is registered as a supplier with the State of Delaware, allowing it to supply wines on behalf of other importers and wine companies to any of Delaware’s licensed alcohol wholesalers/distributers.

Piceno LLC has numerous wine labels (see “Vineyards” for partial listing) approved by the United States federal government for import and sale in the U.S. market.

On its own and in conjunction with other distributers, Piceno LLC wines appear in the following states: Delaware, Massachusetts, Pennsylvania, and Rhode Island.
